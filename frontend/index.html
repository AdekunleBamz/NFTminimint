FAQ

Everything you need

Is NFTminimint production-ready?

The smart contract uses OpenZeppelin libraries and is optimized for secure minting. The frontend is a reference template to accelerate your launch.

Can I customize mint fees?

Yes. Adjust the fee in the contract and mirror it in your UI settings.

How do I deploy?

Use Hardhat scripts and configure your desired network in hardhat.config.js.

Can I use IPFS?

Absolutely. Point the metadata URI to IPFS gateways or direct CIDs.
